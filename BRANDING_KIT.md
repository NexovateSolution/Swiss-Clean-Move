## Visual Style Guide

### Photography Style
- **Clean & Bright**: Well-lit, professional spaces
- **Before/After**: Show transformation results
- **Team Photos**: Professional, uniformed staff
- **Swiss Elements**: Subtle Swiss flag or cross elements
- **Action Shots**: Team at work, equipment in use

### Graphic Elements
- **Swiss Cross**: Used as accent element
- **Clean Lines**: Minimal, professional design
- **Icons**: Consistent service icons across platforms
- **Gradients**: Blue-to-green Swiss gradient
- **White Space**: Clean, uncluttered layouts

7. **Analytics**: Track performance and adjust strategy accordingly
